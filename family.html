The family

Family is one of the universal talking points, it may not be what you are going to talk about the minute you are 
introduced to someone, but it won't be long before you end up sharing stories that include your members. Family or 
listening to the stories of others. It may also be that, in addition to hearing about someone's family, they invite 
you to meet them. This is why family members are a basic topic when it comes to learning English.
